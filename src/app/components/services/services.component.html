Our Services

Varsity Unlimited Tutors Provides the following services

  • Assignment Guidelines

    We help students unlock the puzzles surrounding their assignments by assisting them to understand requirements. This is done through the provision of bulleted guidelines to each assignment question. More personalised assignment help is available from VUT on request. It should also be noted that once the assignment has been solved and guidelines provided then you can call VUT anytime for clarity and further explanations.

  • Assignment Model Answers

    This is a closer and a further step from the one explained above. Sometimes students would want to see how the different facts are synthesised and interlinked to produce a flowing essay. Students should however note that they are not to submit the model answers just as they are as this will amount to plagiarism, but they should change these to be their own product.

  • Project Proposal Guidelines

    Most students do not know how to write project proposals and such assistance is offered. We offer our help from the very first step of choosing a topic for your research. We have a very rich database with research topics, we do not prescribe to students the research topic to choose but we do offer guidance looking at the advantages and disadvantages of any topic.

  • Dissertation Writing Guidance

    Guidance is given from chapter one until the final product is finished. We mark also completed work and give suggestions, as well as checking for plagiarism. Dissertations are quite strenuous and cumbersome. Believe me students, you cannot this walk this path alone, you need VUT’s helping hand.

  • Group Seminars

    If students that are stationed in the same area want some clarity on a certain topic in a certain module VUT will send an expert to clear all the confusion. This can be over a weekend from Friday through Sunday. These are almost the same as your Group lessons but seminars can be on VUT’s chosen topic and they will be announced in advance via the Website. Students then register online and any burning questions surrounding the Topic will be answered.

  • Skype Video/Voice Lessons

    This is offered to modules that require calculations mainly. An expert in the field takes the student through the questions one after another. The questions should have been forwarded to VUT in advance. Once you have given your assignment to VUT and guidelines have been provided and you have paid for that service, skype and voice lessons become free of charge. You will have unlimited access to a specialist regarding that particular module whose guidelines you would have received. VUT boasts of many students you never attended lectures but were taught via the internet through skype and they got flying colours.

  • Workshops and Groups Lessons

    This can be done for the whole module to learners in a particular area. For example if there are people who are doing Managerial Finance in Mpumalanga, they form a study group of not less than 3 people. Once this is done they forward their requests Varsity Unlimited Tutors as in difficult topics they would want help on. Varsity Unlimited tutors hold these workshops on Friday Saturday and Sunday. This time is chosen not to interfere with your work. VUT sends specialists anywhere is SA, the bigger the group the better as the sum will be shared among many students.
